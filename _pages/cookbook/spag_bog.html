Tofu spag bog

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 brown onion, diced
  • 1-2 sticks celery, diced
  • 1 med carrot, diced
  • 2 cl garlic, crushed
  • Thyme, oregano and/or rosemary
  • Tofu for four, grated/shredded/.../frying in a tbsp neutral oil ahead is always a good idea
  • 1 tin toms
  • Tom paste/passata (optional)
  • 1/3 cp Aldi's crappiest dry white
  • 1 cp veg stock (or the powdered stuff)
  • 1-2 tsp vegemite (or marmite or Bovril if you don't mind that it's meaty)
  • 1/2-1 tsp nutmeg
  • Pasta for four
  • Cheese to finish

Instructions

  1. Fry off mirepoix in a little olive oil, add herbs and garlic, add tofu and continue to cook off.
  2. When you're happy with where that's at, add wet things: toms, dry white, stock, vegemite dissolved in 1/2 cp water, nutmeg and season with black pepp. Start ya pasta water.
  3. Do the pasta.
  4. Finish sauce with pasta water and finish pasta in the sauce.
  5. Finish with cheese + pepp!

Notes

Start small on the vegemite friendo.
